MSBTE to organise re-exam for those who missed it
due to rains

Pune, 30th September 2021: Maharashtra Higher and Technical Education Minister Uday Samant has informed that students who missed the MHT-CET examination due to heavy rains can now appear for the same. The State government has decided to conduct the CET again on October 9 and 10th. Many regions of the state especially Marathwada received heavy rains on Tuesday and Wednesday. Many areas got flooded and prevented many students from reaching exam centres. As these students have already paid the examination fees, they will be registered free of cost. The additional examinations of PCM and PCB Group will be held on October 9 and October 10 by providing new admission cards to the registered candidates as per the availability of examination centres.
